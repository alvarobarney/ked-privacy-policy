Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the KED (Kodiak Electric Drives) mobile application ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our field-service management tool. The App is intended for internal use by authorized Kodiak Electric Drives personnel only.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account credentials — your username and password, stored securely on your device using the platform's encrypted secure storage and never transmitted in plain text.
  • Location data — GPS coordinates are accessed only when you use map features or check-in to a unit in the field. Location is not tracked in the background.
  • Work data — unit records, work orders, preventive maintenance logs, part change history, and comments you create within the App. This data is stored locally on your device and synced to our secure Supabase database.
  • Biometric data — if you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint / Face ID), the authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We never receive or store biometric data.
  • Device information — basic app version and platform information used for support and debugging purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and grant access to the App.
  • To store and sync field-service records across devices used by your team.
  • To display unit locations on the in-app map.
  • To improve App reliability and troubleshoot issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database and synced to a Supabase-hosted Postgres database secured with row-level security policies. Data in transit is protected by TLS. Credentials are stored using platform secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

4. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as your account is active or as required for business operations. Records may be soft-deleted (marked inactive) rather than permanently removed to preserve audit history.

5. Your Rights

As an authorized user you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting your system administrator. Deletion requests will be honored within 30 days where not prohibited by operational or legal requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication backend.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tile rendering (no account or API key required).

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
